-> Architecture Enhancement: Squeeze-and-Excitation (SE) Blocks.

Why? SE blocks explicitly model channel interdependencies. They allow the network to perform feature recalibration (learning "which channels are more important"), improving representational power with minimal parameter increase.


Training Enhancement: MixUp Augmentation.


Why? MixUp trains the network on linear combinations of pairs of examples and their labels. This encourages the network to behave linearly in-between training examples, reducing overfitting and improving robustness.


Regularization: Cutout (Está incluída na categoria "Erasure-based augmentations").

Why? Randomly masking out square regions of the input during training forces the model to focus on less prominent features rather than relying on a specific visual cue (like a dog's ear).
